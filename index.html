Simple Lists

Unordered list

Here's a list of information that is presented in bullet points without any particliar order of importance. For example, beverages people drink in the morning:

  • Tea
  • Cola
  • Orange Juice
  • Coffee

Ordered list

In this list, the order of the items is important. For example, the world's top five hottest peppers (as of Dec. 31, 2013 according to Top Food Facts):

  1. Trinidad Moruga Scorpion
  2. Bhut Jolokia
  3. Red Savina Habanero Pepper
  4. Habanero Chilli
  5. Scotch Bonnet
